Pork Ginaling

Pork Ginaling is sauted ground pork with a mix of veggies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Ground Pork
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 shallots
  • 1 whole potato
  • 1 whole carrot
  • soy sauce
  • oyster sauce
  • salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Process

  1. Mince garlic, shallots
  2. dice your potatoes and carrots into cubes
  3. Saute garlic and shallots in a little oil
  4. Add in your ground pork
  5. Season with salt and Pepper
  6. Add in Soy sauce and oyster sauce to give it color
  7. Once the fat has been rendered out of the pork, add the potatoes and carrots
  8. Mix and let them cook for 5 mins or until the potatoes are cooked
  9. Mix into the dish some freshly squeezed calamansi for that sour and tangy taste
