Pork Sinigang

Pork Sinigang is best known for its sour soup and tender pork

Ingredients

  • 2 kg Pork Spare Ribs
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 2 shallots
  • 1 whole bunch of Cabbage or Bok Choy
  • 1 pack of Sinigang Mix
  • 3 pcs Sili Espada

Cooking Process

  1. Cut up the Pork Ribs into 2-inch pieces
  2. place your garlic and onions in one pot
  3. place in Pork Ribs in Pot as well
  4. Fill pot with water
  5. mix in 1 pack of sinigang mix
  6. Bring water to boil. When at a boil, bring down your fire to medium low
  7. Let pork simmer until tender
  8. once pork is tender, add in your sili espada, cabbage or bok choy
